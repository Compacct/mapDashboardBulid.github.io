Factory

SBD ARKAY ENGINEERS (P) LTD was founded in 1987 and is one of the leading manufacturers of Fiber Glass Covered, Cotton covered, Paper Covered, Nomex Covered, Polyester Film with Fiber Glass covering, Mica Film Covered, Super Enameled Fiber Glass Covered Copper and Aluminium Wires.

Factory

We have reputation of supplying quality products to reputed OEMs of Electrical Equipments with good presence in the Indian Market. Our entire range of products are manufactured as per Indian Standards and specifications. We have most modern facilities and the experience for manufacturing for our entire range of products.
